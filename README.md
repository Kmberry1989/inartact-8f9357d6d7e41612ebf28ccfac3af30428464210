2. **Nov 28 Launch**: Right after the information is loaded into the site
3. **Nov 28 Distribution**: print flyer/web flyer
    - Print flyers - take to the Student Life Dept or Media&Marketing at everyone’s home campus to get approved to hang, hang the flyers
    - Social Media Creative- Post on the Kokomo Art Association Facebook (where Rochelle works) and all of our personal Facebook pages, possibly any other local art associations who may be interested in sharing as well.

## Goal
To inspire others to create change in Indiana through art and influence the way others think about present-day issues, with an informative website dedicated to Indiana art activists and their contributions.
